Tour the UNDERGROUND with your travel buddy, Chara! Between sibling rivalry, casual murder, and a constant looming threat of death, you'll finally be able to scratch that itch for more.

NIHILISM is a what-if scenario taking place after at least one Pacifist and one Genocide run which explores Chara and Flowey's characters more in-depth. It's packed full of new encounters, interactions, areas, and fun and exciting moments that feel straight out of UNDERTALE.
